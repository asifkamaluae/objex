WHY OBJEX EXISTS

The world's oldest financial system, finally unbreakable.

For centuries, billions have pooled money in rotating savings circles — ROSCAs, tandas, susus, hui, committees. Members contribute every month; each takes a turn receiving the whole pot. It is how most of the world actually builds capital — an estimated $500B+ moves through these circles every year.

They share one fatal flaw: trust. The moment a member is paid out early and walks away, the circle collapses. For a thousand years there was no fix. Programmable smart contracts are the fix — and they turn idle savings into productive trading capital.
